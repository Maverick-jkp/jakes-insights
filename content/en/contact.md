# Contact

We welcome topic suggestions, error corrections, and feedback.

---

## How to Reach Us

**GitHub Issues** (preferred)
For bug reports, factual corrections, or topic suggestions:
[github.com/Maverick-jkp/jakes-insights/issues](https://github.com/Maverick-jkp/jakes-insights/issues)

**RSS Feed**
Subscribe to stay updated:
[jakeinsight.com/index.xml](/index.xml)

---

## What We Respond To

- **Factual corrections** — If something in an article is wrong, please open a GitHub issue with a source. We correct errors promptly.
- **Topic suggestions** — Working on something interesting? Noticed a tech story we haven't covered? Let us know.
- **Broken links or images** — Report via GitHub Issues.

---

## What We Don't Accept

- Guest post requests
- Sponsored content or paid placements
- Link exchange requests
- SEO service pitches

---

*Response time: 1–3 business days via GitHub Issues.*
