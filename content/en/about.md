# About Jake's Insights

**Jake's Insights** is a bilingual technology publication covering AI, developer tools, cloud infrastructure, and cybersecurity — written for engineers who want analysis, not headlines.

---

## The Author

**Jake Park** is a software developer and tech writer based in Seoul, Korea. He writes about the practical realities of building software in 2026: which tools actually deliver, where AI falls short, and what the engineering community is debating right now.

Jake monitors developer communities — HackerNews, Dev.to, Lobsters — daily, translating the most relevant discussions into structured analysis for both English and Korean readers.

## Editorial Approach

**Community-sourced topics** — We track what working engineers are actually discussing, not what PR agencies pitch.

**Data-backed claims** — Every cost figure, benchmark, and statistic is cited. We don't speculate.

**No fluff** — Articles are 900–1,800 words. Long enough to be useful, short enough to finish.

**Bilingual** — Full English and Korean editions. Korean articles are written for Korean developers, not translated word-for-word.
