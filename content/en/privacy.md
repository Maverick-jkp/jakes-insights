# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date**: February 27, 2026
**Last Updated**: February 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Jake's Insights (jakeinsight.com) collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Analytics Data
We use **Google Analytics 4** to understand how visitors use our site. Data collected includes:
- Pages visited and time spent
- Referral source (how you found us)
- Geographic location (country/region only)
- Device type, browser, and operating system
- Session behavior (bounce rate, scroll depth)

This data is aggregated and anonymized. We do not receive personally identifiable information from Google Analytics.

### Advertising Data
We display advertisements through **Google AdSense**. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your browsing history across sites. This is governed by Google's advertising policies.

### Affiliate Links
Some articles contain affiliate links to products on **Amazon** and **Coupang**. When you click an affiliate link and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. Affiliate links are disclosed in the relevant articles.

### No Direct Data Collection
We do **not** operate contact forms, newsletter signups, or user accounts. We do not directly collect names, email addresses, or other personal identifiers.

## 2. Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

| Cookie Type | Provider | Purpose |
|-------------|----------|---------|

Depending on your location, you may have rights under **GDPR** (EU/EEA) or **CCPA** (California):

- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your data
